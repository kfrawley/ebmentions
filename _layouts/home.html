I am very lonely and entirely yours,

Marie Mancini's (1639-1715) unpublished letters to Lorenzo Colonna (her estranged husband) and others reside in the Colonna Archive at the Library of the Abbey of Santa Scolastica, in Subiaco, Italy. The Letters of Marie Mancini is an initiative to transcribe, translate, visualize, and make them available to the public.
